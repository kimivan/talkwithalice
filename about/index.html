I'm Alice Kim

I'm a Brazilian-Korean-American Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) and National Certified Counselor (NCC) based in Atlanta, GA.

I profoundly believe in the transformative power of psychotherapy and the strength within every individual to create meaningful change in their life. With a foundation of honesty, compassion, authenticity, inclusivity, and growth, I approach every session with professionalism and care. I am committed to helping you achieve both personal insight and lasting change, supporting you on your journey to a healthier, more balanced life.

Growing up with diverse cultural influences, I know firsthand how culture, identity, and personal experiences shape who we are. This perspective allows me to empathize with clients from all backgrounds, especially those navigating cultural adjustment, identity exploration, or balancing multiple identities. As a therapist, I prioritize creating a safe, non-judgmental space where you can process your experiences and explore the many layers of your well-being.

When I'm not in session, I enjoy spending time with family and friends, writing music, traveling, and indulging in a delicious plate of pasta.

My Approach

My goal as a therapist isn't just to 'fix' problems but to help you gain deeper insight into your emotions, relationships, and behaviors.

I'm committed to offering treatment options that are uniquely suited to your goals. Together, we'll explore your challenges and develop actionable solutions using a blend of evidence-based therapies tailored to your needs.

I incorporate a range of evidence-based therapies, including EMDR, Psychodynamic Therapy, Person-Centered Therapy, Somatic Therapy, Narrative Therapy, Motivational Interviewing, Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT), and Mindfulness. Each of these approaches offers valuable tools to help you process emotions, shift unhelpful patterns, and build a stronger, more fulfilling life.

Areas of Expertise

Depression Therapy

If you're feeling overwhelmed by sadness or hopelessness, therapy can help uncover the root causes and guide you toward healing and relief.

Anxiety and Stress Therapy

If anxiety is taking over your life, we'll work together to identify triggers, manage stress, and develop tools to regain control and find calm.

EMDR and Trauma Therapy

I specialize in EMDR and trauma-informed therapy, helping you heal from past traumas and reduce their impact on your daily life.

Grief Counseling

Grief can feel isolating, but therapy offers a safe space to process your emotions and find a path toward healing.

Identity Exploration

Balancing multiple identities can be complex. Together, we'll explore your experiences and help you embrace your true self with confidence and clarity.

Family and Relationship Dynamics

I can help you navigate family dynamics and relationships, improving communication and fostering healthier, more meaningful connections.

Individual Counseling

If you're unsure where to start, we can work together to boost your self-confidence, identify your goals, and create a clear path forward.
