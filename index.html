How I Can Help

Are you feeling stuck, overwhelmed, or unsure of what's going wrong? In our sessions, you'll find a safe, judgment-free space to explore your emotions and gain valuable insights.

I blend empathy with proven therapeutic techniques such as EMDR, CBT, and mindfulness, tailoring our work together to meet your unique needs.

Together, we'll uncover what's standing in your way and create a path forward with confidence and clarity.

A Little About Me

As a Brazilian-Korean-American therapist, I deeply understand how culture and identity shape our lives. My multicultural background allows me to empathize with clients from diverse backgrounds, offering a culturally aware and inclusive approach to therapy.

Therapy is a safe space to explore your emotions without judgment. I create a warm, welcoming environment where you can feel comfortable discussing the most difficult aspects of your life.

Areas of Expertise

Anxiety and Stress Therapy
