A Little About Me

I'm Alice Kim, a Brazilian-Korean-American Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) and National Certified Counselor (NCC) based in Atlanta, GA.

I hold a Master of Arts in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Northwestern University and a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from Georgia State University.

I'm committed to helping clients navigate life’s toughest challenges, from stress and anxiety to trauma and identity exploration. My approach is grounded in empathy and evidence-based therapies tailored to your unique experiences.

As a Brazilian-Korean-American, I bring a nuanced cultural perspective to therapy. I understand how the intersection of different identities can shape your experiences. In our work together, you’ll find a compassionate space where we explore these layers, empowering you to embrace your authentic self.

My Services

  • Depression Therapy

    Feeling overwhelmed or stuck by sadness? Therapy is the first step toward reclaiming your life. We’ll uncover what’s weighing you down and explore small, impactful changes that can bring relief. Let’s start your healing journey today.

  • Anxiety & Stress Therapy

    Anxiety can feel like you’re on constant edge—racing thoughts, worry, or an unshakeable sense of unease. Together, we’ll identify the root causes of your stress and develop tools to ground you. Let’s help you regain control and live with more calm.

  • Trauma & EMDR Therapy

    Past trauma can affect your emotions and relationships. If you’re ready to heal, we can work through those painful experiences using EMDR and other trauma-informed techniques. Together, we’ll reduce the hold of the past, so you can feel more in control and at peace.

  • Grief Counseling

    Grief can be unpredictable, leaving you feeling lost or isolated. Whether you’re mourning a person, a relationship, or a life change, therapy provides a safe space to process your feelings. Together, we’ll honor your loss and explore a path forward that feels authentic and healing.

  • Identity Exploration

    Balancing multiple identities—whether cultural, immigrant, or personal—can be complex. As a Brazilian-Korean-American therapist, I understand how these experiences shape who you are. In therapy, we’ll explore the many layers of your identity, helping you embrace your true self with confidence.

  • Family and Relationships Counseling

    Family dynamics, relationships, and communication issues can create tension and frustration. In therapy, we’ll explore these challenges, improve understanding, and build stronger connections. Whether you’re navigating family conflict or relationship struggles, we’ll work toward healthier, more meaningful bonds.

  • Individual Counseling

    Life can be complicated—sometimes you just need space to reflect, process, and move forward. In individual counseling, we’ll work through challenges, build resilience, and help you create the life you want. Together, we’ll focus on what truly matters to you and build a path forward that feels aligned with your values.

How I Can Help

Therapy is a personal journey, and I’m here to guide you with compassion and expertise.

My goal isn’t to simply ‘fix’ problems but to help you gain deep insight into your emotions, relationships, and behaviors. Together, we’ll work toward meaningful change using effective, evidence-based therapies tailored to your needs, such as:

  • EMDR
  • Psychodynamic Therapy
  • Person-Centered Therapy
  • Somatic Therapy
  • Narrative Therapy
  • Motivational Interviewing
  • Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Mindfulness

I’m committed to offering you the best treatment options tailored to your specific goals, and I will work alongside you every step of the way to help you achieve them.
