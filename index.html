How I Can Help

If you're feeling stuck, overwhelmed, or unsure of what's going wrong—especially when it feels like you’ve tried everything—you’re not alone.

It can be incredibly frustrating when you've tried to handle things on your own, yet still feel like something isn’t working. I’m here to provide the guidance and tools you need to move forward with confidence and clarity.

I specialize in supporting individuals through life’s most complex challenges, including:

  • Healing from trauma and its lasting impact
  • Managing anxiety, depression and struggle
  • Navigating life transitions and cultural adjustments
  • Working through identity-related challenges
  • Processing grief and loss
  • Addressing family dynamics and relationship concerns

I believe healing happens when we truly understand ourselves, which is why I approach therapy through a compassionate lens, shaped by my own experiences as an immigrant with a multicultural background.

A Little About Me

I'm Alice Kim, a Brazilian-Korean-American Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) and National Certified Counselor (NCC) based in Atlanta, GA.

I hold a Master of Arts in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Northwestern University and a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from Georgia State University.

As someone with a multicultural background, I bring a nuanced cultural perspective to therapy. I understand how the intersection of different identities can shape your experiences. In our work together, you’ll find a compassionate space where we explore these layers, empowering you to embrace your authentic self.
