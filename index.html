How I Can Help

Are you feeling stuck, overwhelmed, or unsure of what's wrong? In our sessions, you'll find a safe, judgment-free space to explore your emotions, relationships, and behaviors with deeper insight.

I blend empathy with evidence-based techniques—such as EMDR, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Psychodynamic, Person-Centered, Somatic, Narrative, Motivational Interviewing, and mindfulness—to tailor our work to your unique needs.

I profoundly believe in the transformative power of psychotherapy and am committed to helping you achieve lasting change. Together, we'll explore how past experiences may shape your current behavior, deepen your awareness of the mind-body connection, and develop new thought patterns and practices to support a more balanced, fulfilling life.

A Little About Me

I'm a Brazilian-Korean-American Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) and National Certified Counselor (NCC) based in Atlanta, GA.

Growing up with diverse cultural influences, I know firsthand how culture, identity, and personal experiences shape who we are. This perspective allows me to empathize with clients from diverse backgrounds, offering a culturally aware and inclusive approach to therapy.

I hold an M.A. in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Northwestern University and a B.A. in Psychology from Georgia State University.

When I'm not in session, I enjoy spending time with family and friends, writing music, traveling, and indulging in a delicious plate of pasta.

Areas of Expertise

  • EMDR & Trauma Therapy

    I specialize in EMDR and trauma-informed therapy, helping you heal from past traumas and reduce their impact on your daily life.

  • Anxiety & Stress Therapy

    If anxiety is taking over your life, we'll work together to identify triggers, manage stress, and develop tools to regain control and find calm.

  • Depression Therapy

    If you're feeling overwhelmed by sadness or hopelessness, therapy can help uncover the root causes and guide you toward healing and relief.

  • Racial Trauma Therapy

    Racial stress and discrimination can take an emotional toll. Together, we'll process these experiences, build resilience, and reclaim your sense of empowerment.

  • Grief Counseling

    Grief can feel isolating, but therapy offers a safe space to process your emotions and find a path toward healing.

  • Perfectionism

    The pressure to be perfect can be overwhelming. We'll work together to break unhelpful patterns, manage self-criticism, and find balance.

  • Self-Esteem

    If self-doubt is holding you back, therapy can help you build confidence, develop self-compassion, and embrace your worth.

  • Family & Relationship Dynamics

    I can help you navigate family dynamics and relationships, improving communication and fostering healthier, more meaningful connections.

  • Identity Exploration

    Balancing multiple identities can be complex. Together, we'll explore your experiences and help you embrace your true self with confidence and clarity.
