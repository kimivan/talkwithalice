A Little About Me

I'm Alice Kim, a Brazilian-Korean-American Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) and National Certified Counselor (NCC) based in Atlanta, GA.

I hold a Master of Arts in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Northwestern University and a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from Georgia State University.

Becoming a therapist has been my lifelong dream. I deeply believe in the transformative power of psychotherapy—while the journey can be challenging, it’s also profoundly rewarding. My mission is to help clients discover their inner strength, heal from within, and achieve meaningful growth.

I bring a culturally rich perspective to therapy, prioritizing honesty, compassion, authenticity, and inclusivity in every session. With professionalism and genuine care, I strive to foster personal transformation and create a safe space where you feel heard, understood, and empowered.

I Help Individuals With

  • Depression

    Feeling weighed down by sadness or stuck in a cycle of low energy? You’re not alone. Therapy offers a chance to explore what’s behind these feelings and make small, meaningful changes to lighten the load. We’ll work at a pace that feels comfortable, focusing on what matters most to you. Let’s start creating a life that feels lighter and more hopeful.

  • Anxiety & Stress

    Anxiety can feel like your mind’s stuck on overdrive—worrying about what’s next, feeling tense, or struggling to relax. In therapy, we’ll figure out what’s fueling these feelings and develop tools to help you feel grounded and calm, no matter what comes your way. Ready to feel more at ease? Let’s talk.

  • Trauma & EMDR

    Trauma doesn’t have to define your story. Whether it’s an event or patterns from the past, therapy can help you process those experiences in a way that feels safe and manageable. Using EMDR and other trauma-informed techniques, we’ll work to help you heal and feel more in control of your life. Let’s rewrite how the past shows up in your present.

  • Grief

    Grief is complex—it might hit you in waves or linger quietly in the background. Whether you’re mourning a person, relationship, or major life change, therapy gives you space to process your feelings and find a way to carry the loss without being consumed by it. Together, we’ll honor your loss and work toward healing.

  • Identity Exploration

    Finding your place in the world can feel complicated, especially when you’re balancing cultural expectations, immigrant experiences, or intersecting identities. As a Brazilian-Korean-American therapist, I get it. In therapy, we’ll explore these layers together, so you can embrace your authentic self with confidence. Let’s navigate your unique path with clarity and care.

  • Family and Relationships

    Relationships can be messy—whether it’s family, friends, or partners, miscommunication and tension can leave you feeling disconnected. Therapy offers a safe space to work through those challenges, rebuild trust, and create stronger, healthier connections. Let’s untangle what’s not working and find a better way forward.

  • Individual Counseling

    Life’s pressures—whether from work, relationships, or personal expectations—can feel overwhelming. Therapy is your space to pause, reflect, and figure out what truly matters to you. Together, we’ll build resilience and create a life that aligns with your values and goals. Let’s work together to build the life you deserve.

How I Can Help

My goal isn’t to “fix” your problems or simply talk you out of your emotions.

Instead, I aim to guide you in understanding them, helping you uncover their root causes to gain deeper insight into yourself, your relationships, and your behavior patterns.

I draw from evidence-based approaches such as:

  • EMDR
  • Psychodynamic Therapy
  • Person-Centered Therapy
  • Somatic Therapy
  • Narrative Therapy
  • Motivational Interviewing
  • Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Mindfulness

As a Brazilian-Korean-American, I have a deep understanding of how identity, culture, and generational expectations shape our experiences. I provide a warm, non-judgmental space for you to process your emotions and develop new tools for growth.

I’m committed to offering you the best treatment options tailored to your specific goals, and I will work alongside you every step of the way to help you achieve them.
