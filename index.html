A Little About Me

Hi, I'm Alice Kim, a Brazilian-Korean-American Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) and National Certified Counselor (NCC) based in Atlanta, GA.

I hold a Master of Arts in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Northwestern University and a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from Georgia State University.

Becoming a therapist has been a lifelong dream of mine, and I have a deep belief in the transformative power of psychotherapy. While the journey can be challenging, it is incredibly rewarding. I am committed to supporting my clients in discovering their inner strength, guiding them towards growth and healing from within.

I prioritize honesty, compassion, authenticity, and inclusivity in my work. Above all, I approach each session with professionalism, care, and a genuine desire to foster personal transformation.

I Help Individuals With

  • Depression

    Living with depression can feel heavy and overwhelming. If you’re navigating deep sadness, low energy, or a sense of hopelessness, you don’t have to face it alone. In therapy, we’ll explore the unique challenges you’re experiencing and work together to uncover small, meaningful changes that bring relief. This is a space for healing and rediscovery, at a pace that feels right for you.

  • Anxiety & Stress

    Anxiety can show up in many ways—racing thoughts, constant worry, or a lingering sense of unease. It can feel like you’re always on edge, waiting for the next challenge. Therapy offers a chance to step back, understand what’s driving your anxiety, and find practical ways to manage it. My goal is to help you feel more grounded, calm, and equipped to handle life’s uncertainties.

  • Trauma & EMDR

    The impact of trauma can linger, affecting your emotions, relationships, and sense of safety. Therapy can help you process these experiences in a way that feels manageable. Using EMDR and other trauma-informed techniques, we’ll work together to reduce the hold past events have on your present. This is a space to heal, rebuild, and move toward a life where you feel more in control.

  • Grief

    Grief is a deeply personal experience that can feel unpredictable and isolating. Whether you’re grieving a loved one, a relationship, or a significant life change, therapy provides a space to process your feelings without judgment. Together, we’ll honor your loss, explore its impact, and help you find a way forward that feels authentic and healing.

  • Identity Exploration

    Exploring your identity can bring a mix of excitement, curiosity, and uncertainty. As a Brazilian-Korean-American, I understand how cultural influences, immigrant experiences, and generational expectations can shape who you are. Whether you’re reflecting on gender, sexuality, cultural background, or navigating the complexities of being part of multiple worlds, therapy provides a space to explore these layers. Together, we’ll process your experiences and empower you to embrace your authentic self with confidence and clarity.

  • Family and Relationships

    Family and relationships are central to our lives, but they can also bring challenges. Miscommunication, conflict, and unresolved issues can create tension that feels hard to untangle. Therapy provides a space to explore these dynamics, improve understanding, and build healthier ways of relating. Whether you're navigating family struggles, a partnership, or broader relationship concerns, we’ll work toward strengthening your connections and fostering meaningful change.

  • Individual Counseling

    Life can feel complicated, and it’s not always easy to navigate on your own. Individual therapy is a chance to reflect, gain insight, and work through challenges in a space that’s entirely your own. Whether you’re feeling stuck, struggling with self-esteem, or overwhelmed by perfectionism, I’m here to help. Together, we’ll explore your experiences, build resilience, and take steps toward the life you want to create—one that feels meaningful and true to you.

How I Can Help

My goal isn’t to “fix” your problems or simply talk you out of your emotions.

Instead, I aim to guide you in understanding them, helping you uncover their root causes to gain deeper insight into yourself, your relationships, and your behavior patterns.

I draw from evidence-based approaches such as:

  • EMDR
  • Psychodynamic Therapy
  • Person-Centered Therapy
  • Somatic Therapy
  • Narrative Therapy
  • Motivational Interviewing
  • Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Mindfulness

As a Brazilian-Korean-American, I have a deep understanding of how identity, culture, and generational expectations shape our experiences. I provide a warm, non-judgmental space for you to process your emotions and develop new tools for growth.

I’m committed to offering you the best treatment options tailored to your specific goals, and I will work alongside you every step of the way to help you achieve them.
